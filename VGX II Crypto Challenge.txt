VGX Crypto Challenge
4 unique exercises progressing in difficulty

Please review the VGX cipher code, readme, and usage text files for information that may help you complete the challenges. 
Plaintexts are human-readable ASCII messages. Before encryption, messages are encoded as hexadecimal so the cipher operates on bytes.
Each encrypted message has a known plaintext header (8 bytes): 3030303030303030 (ASCII "00000000")
Hex encoding is part of the plaintext and is not an encryption layer. Recovered plaintexts should be interpreted as ASCII after hex decoding.
